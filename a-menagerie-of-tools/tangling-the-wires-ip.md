# Failing the Network \(\`ip\`\)

The `ip` tool lets you manage network links on your system. It also allows you to manipulate some settings related to the links.

Typically, your main Ethernet link is `eth0`, along with a loopback on `lo`.If it's not you can use `ip address` to discover which link is of interest to you.
